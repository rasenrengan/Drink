Our Legacy of Quality

Established 1923

For over a century, DRINK has been a trusted and reliable name in the community. Serving Hurghada since 1923, we take immense pride in our heritage as purveyors of fine wines and select beers.

Our commitment to quality has remained unchanged through the decades. We curate only the most premium selections to ensure that every bottle tells a story of excellence. Thank you for making us a part of your celebrations for generations.
